Our Company


Few companies employ a full-time workspace installer. That is where Valley Office Installations can help. We keep businesses operating efficiently through relocations, reorganizations, updating furniture, adding cubicles and more. Valley Office Installations will be onsite to manage installations, reconfigurations and relocations.



Meeting our expectations is not easy but Valley View Installations lived up to their name.


Serving Clients since 2010

2010 is the year we began offering services in the Rogue Valley. Since that time we have helped more than a thousand companies move, install or reconfigure their offices. When it comes to logistics, office installations or reconfigurations then Valley Office Installations is a company you can trust.

Our staff is trained in the installation of many systems including Herman Miller, Stellcase, Hawsworth, Knoll, Hon, Haworth, Kimbal, National, Global and more, we work with them all.

Our Services

We provide logistic services including airfright, ocean fright, road transport, office installations and reconfigurations. If you need to move your offices, then Valley Office Installations is who to call. We are experts in furniture systems and the design of workspace products. Let our professional team help you plan the installation or reconfiguration of your offices.

Our team is trained in installation, reconfigurations, and setup of modular furniture.
